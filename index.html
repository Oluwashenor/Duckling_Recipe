Easy Pancake Recipe

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 20 mins
Yields: 4 Servings
For Beginners

01.

Sieve your flour into a bowl, add your baking powder, your salt, and then your sugar.

02.

Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl, set aside.

03.

Crack your eggs into a different bowl, add your milk, and whisk together. If you want a lighter texture, add a little water.

04.

Create a well in the flour bowl, and pour in the egg mix. Stir gently, it will start to bubble a bit.

05.

Heat your butter up in a non stick pan, scoop a bit of the pancake batter, and add to the pan. Flip the pancake when it starts to bubble at the top.
